🎯 Content Pillars & Strategy

🎬 Reels — Reach & Discovery

  • Hook in first 1.5 seconds — show destination magic
  • Under 30 seconds for max completion rate
  • Use trending audio for algorithmic boost
  • "Stop scrolling if you want to visit…" format
  • Post Mon, Wed, Fri, Sat for optimal reach

📖 Carousels — Education & Saves

  • Hook slide with bold text/question
  • 8–10 slides for maximum swipe-through
  • End with clear CTA ("Save this for your trip")
  • Mix images + short clips per slide
  • Tuesday & Thursday — best save rates

⚡ Stories & Engagement

  • Daily stories: polls, quizzes, countdowns
  • "Would you rather" destination polls
  • Trip sneak-peek BTS content
  • Reply to every comment for first hour
  • Collab posts with micro travel influencers

🏷️ Hashtag Strategy

  • 5–10 hashtags per post (2026 best practice)
  • #TripzygoTravel (own branded hashtag)
  • Mix: #LuxuryTravel #IndiaTravel #GroupTrips
  • Destination-specific: #KashmirTrip #BaliTravel
  • Niche: #CustomisedHolidays #AITravel
